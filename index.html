An easy spinach lasagna recipe that saves me lots of time

This recipe is simple and Popeye would love it

Ingredients

  • 12oz baby spinach
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 8 lasagna noodles

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Bake for 45 minutes
